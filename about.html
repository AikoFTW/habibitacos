Our Story

The journey of bringing Middle Eastern and Mexican flavors together in Seattle

Where Middle East Meets Mexico

Born in the heart of Seattle, Habibi Tacos brings together the bold flavors of Mexico with the rich spices and traditions of the Arab world. Our mission is simple: create delicious, authentic halal fusion dishes that celebrate the beautiful intersection of cultures.

Every dish tells a story of heritage, community, and the incredible diversity that makes Seattle special. From our signature shawarma tacos to our aromatic rice bowls, we're bringing something completely new to the Pacific Northwest food scene.

We believe food is the universal language that brings people together, and our fusion concept reflects the beautiful tapestry of Seattle's multicultural community.
